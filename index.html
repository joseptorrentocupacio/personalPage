WORK

CleaNotes

CleaNotes is the final project made last year on Centre d'Estudis Politecnics

It's an Android App to manage your daily notes on your SmartPhone. We implemented what we called rooted notes.

This is a basic functionality that consist on notes inside notes.

You can try this app on Google Play Store.

SnarkyPuppy

Snarky Puppy is a simple scape room simulator

This is a intuitive and user friendly web app, where you can get a tour by Antoni Gaudí's Barcelona, and his darker secrets

You have to play some trick games set on different places like Sagrada Familia or Park Güell

Click on the image or on SnarkyPuppy if you want to see the website.

SPAM

SPAM is the last project I worked for.

The "Societat Protectora d'Animals de Mataró" asked for a simple and intuitive app to manage their donations.

We worked hard to make a simple public part of the web where you can see statistics related on SPAM donations, charts and information about where can you donate almost anything.

Click on the image or on SPAM if you want to see the website.
